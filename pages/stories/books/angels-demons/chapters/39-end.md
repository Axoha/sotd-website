During the late twenty-first century humanity was in...
Peace.

No lives were taken, no sightings occurred, nothing out of the ordinary happened. It was a perfectly normal sunny day on earth. Wars in the outer cosmos however still raged on, as in their nature of greed and conquest between empires. The Bastion never came to be, however from the same species "The Citadel" was created. Interestingly enough, not all evidence of the Holy Earth was erased. Some Spear pilots survived the incomplete reconstruction event, becoming nomads of the cosmos telling stories and legends to whoever was interested. The New Babylon was successfully formed, becoming what could be referred to as the "Good Holy Earth", it held one of the biggest libraries across all existence that contained all the documents and legends anyone could think of. The Angels and Demons watched from afar, working behind the scenes to create life like how they always did - this time however several Angels disguised as the populations they were coming to were sent to keep watch. 
Ferd walked through the busy streets of New York during the rain, his red marks were gray. Without a family, without a home, he held tightly to his glasses that he does not remember the origin of. A tall woman noticed him, leaning down to help the child with a worried face.

"Aw, who could you be? Where are your parents?" She asked, holding her umbrella above them both.

He was silent, his pupils had a white glow. She took a closer look at his collar, noticing a name.

"Ferd..."
